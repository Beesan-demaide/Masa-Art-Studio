My Work

We hope to like my paintings!

heritage

Every artist expresses his environment as it expresses him/her, and the Palestinian heritage is an authentic thing and a mirror that reflects the aesthetics of our people. Therefore, I care about the presence of heritage details in my paintings

women

Women for women, from here I find my duty to paint and show the feminine and highlight the most insomnia in them.

flower

Wherever women are found, there are flowers. I have always felt the tenderness and softness of flowers. I find myself drawing them and learning more about them.

feelings

Drawing means feelings, and there is no more sense and transparency of feelings than drawing
